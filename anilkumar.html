A. P. Anil Kumar (born 15 March 1965) is an Indian politician, social worker, and senior leader of the Indian National Congress from Kerala. Born in Malappuram district to A. P. Balan and Devakiamma, he earned a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and became active in student politics through the Kerala Students Union (KSU). Rising through the ranks of the KSU and the Indian Youth Congress, he established himself as a prominent youth leader in the state. He was first elected to the Kerala Legislative Assembly from the Wandoor constituency in 2001 and has represented the constituency for multiple consecutive terms. Throughout his political career, he has served in several important ministerial roles, including Minister for Welfare of Scheduled Communities, Youth and Cultural Affairs, Tourism, and Backward Communities. Recognized for his involvement in social welfare, tourism development, and community-focused initiatives, he remains an influential figure in Kerala politics. Since 2026, he has been serving as the Minister for Land Revenue and Survey in the Kerala government headed by V. D. Satheesan.
