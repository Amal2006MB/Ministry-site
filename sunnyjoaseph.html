Kannoth Muraleedharan (born 14 May 1957) is an Indian politician, lawyer, and prominent leader of the Indian National Congress from Kerala. Born in Thrissur to former Kerala Chief Minister K. Karunakaran and Kalyanikutty Amma, he studied at St. Joseph's Higher Secondary School, Mar Ivanios College, and Kerala Law Academy Law College. He began his political career through the Congress Seva Dal and gradually rose through the ranks, serving as District Chairman, State Chief of Seva Dal, and later President of the Kerala Pradesh Congress Committee (KPCC). Muraleedharan has represented Kozhikode and Vatakara in the Lok Sabha and Vattiyoorkavu in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. Throughout his career, he has played a significant role in Kerala politics, serving in various leadership positions within the Congress party. In the 2026 Kerala Legislative Assembly election, he won from Vattiyoorkavu and was appointed Minister for Health, Family Welfare, and Devaswoms in the Kerala government.
